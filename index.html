About

I lead the design and delivery of modern Lakehouse platforms that are governed, observable, and ready for Machine Learning and Generative AI workloads. At Databricks I direct architecture programs for Fortune 500 clients including Takeda, Chubb, Centene, CVS, Merck, Grammarly, NYU Langone, and Xcel Energy, covering Unity Catalog migrations, ABAC and PII protection, DLT pipelines, MLOps, and Generative AI accelerators.

Before Databricks I spent 11 years at General Motors leading EDW and Dealer Data Management modernization, with earlier leadership roles at Bank of America and Ford. My focus is clean architecture, data integrity, security, and translating complex business needs into systems that teams can confidently operate at scale.
